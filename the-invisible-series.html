The Invisible Series

Mum cooking a pot full of pasta and spilling alfredo sauce all over the place and making us clean it up cause her hands are busy with all the cooking and us reluctantly using paper towels to wipe the alfredo sauce off the floor.

Blood-warm dead eels strewn across the floor, a termite-chewed, Victorian bed with sheets stained brown, standing alone in the middle and a book with all its pages torn out and stacked neatly onto a pile of old dressing gowns, placed precariously on the edge of a stool.

Limbless mannequins hanging from a ceiling, swaying like pendulums out of phase, with a rat scurrying across carrying a loaf of break and a bottle of wine, while you stare at your own reflection in a mirror, wondering about reality.
